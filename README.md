**Opening in browser**
Start your server by typing into the terminal:
npx serve .

**Controls:**
+: zoom in (all views)
-: zoom out (all views)

  **Camera (Perspective view)**
    Q / E: rotate left / right
    W / S: rotate up / down
    
**Line Tool**
Click once in any viewport: to start your line
Move your mouse: to preview dashed line
Click again: to confirm line

All endpoints will snap to the viewportâ€™s grid.

**Available Viewports**
You can view your drawings from the Top, Front, Right, and Perspective viewports.
The Perspective viewport is the 3D view, whereas the other 3 are 2D views to help you accurately snap your lines in the correct planes.
